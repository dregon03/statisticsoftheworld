## Comment

Solid list for market data. Worth adding one for the macro fundamentals layer: [Statistics of the World](https://statisticsoftheworld.com/api-docs) is what I reach for when I need economic context alongside the market data — GDP, inflation, unemployment, current account balance, debt-to-GDP, etc. for 218 countries, pulled from IMF and World Bank sources.

Honest caveat: it's annual/quarterly data, not real-time. But for anything where you want to show the underlying macro picture alongside price data — EM equities, forex fundamentals, country-risk dashboards — that's usually what you need anyway. Free API, 100 requests/day without authentication, 440+ indicators.

Good complement to the market data APIs you listed when you need to answer "why is the Brazilian real moving" rather than just "how much is it moving".
