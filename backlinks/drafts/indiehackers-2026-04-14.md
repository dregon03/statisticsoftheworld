## Option A: Create a product page

**Product name**: Statistics of the World

**Tagline**: Free API for GDP, population & 440+ economic indicators across 218 countries

**Description**:
Statistics of the World aggregates economic and demographic data from the IMF, World Bank, and WHO into a single normalized API. It's free to use — 100 requests/day without auth, free API key for 1,000/day.

Started this because I kept having to juggle three different API formats whenever I needed cross-country comparisons for a project. One endpoint now covers most of what you'd need: GDP, inflation, unemployment, population, life expectancy, trade balance, CO2 emissions, and 440+ more.

Tech stack: Next.js, Supabase, deployed on Hetzner via Coolify.

Revenue: $0 (free product, building traffic first)

**Website**: https://statisticsoftheworld.com
