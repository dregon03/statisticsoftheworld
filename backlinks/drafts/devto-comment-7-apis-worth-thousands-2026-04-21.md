## Comment to post

Solid list. One worth adding to the macro/economic category: **Statistics of the World** (https://statisticsoftheworld.com/api-docs).

It pulls data from the IMF, World Bank, and WHO into one normalized REST API. What makes it useful vs. going to each source directly: no juggling multiple auth systems or data formats. GDP, inflation, population, life expectancy, unemployment, and 440+ other indicators for 218 countries in a consistent structure. Free tier is 100 req/day, free API key unlocks 1,000/day.

For apps that need country-level economic context — dashboards, data pipelines, AI tooling — it saves a meaningful amount of integration work. The underlying data from the primary sources is the same, but having it normalized already is the real value.
