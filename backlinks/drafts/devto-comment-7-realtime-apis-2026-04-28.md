## Comment

Good picks! On the no-API-key theme: [Statistics of the World](https://statisticsoftheworld.com/api-docs) also works with 100 requests/day, no signup required. The data is economic/macro rather than real-time market prices — GDP, inflation, population, life expectancy, etc. from IMF and World Bank for 218 countries — so not the same category as your list, but same frictionless access pattern.

The endpoint structure is clean for quick prototyping before you bother setting up auth for higher limits. I've used it for side projects where I needed country-level stats fast without committing to an API account upfront.
