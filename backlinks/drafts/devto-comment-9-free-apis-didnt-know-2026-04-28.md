## Comment

Great list! One I'd add that a lot of developers don't know about: [Statistics of the World](https://statisticsoftheworld.com/api-docs). It aggregates data from the IMF, World Bank, WHO, and UN into a single normalized API — so instead of juggling three different query syntaxes when you need GDP + inflation + population for a country, you hit one endpoint and get consistent JSON back.

Free for up to 100 requests/day without even creating an account. Covers 440+ indicators across 218 countries. The World Bank and IMF APIs are the gold standard for the actual data, but their query formats are genuinely painful if you just want a quick country lookup. This normalizes all of that.

Useful for anything involving country-level economic comparisons, demographic data, or macro indicators.
