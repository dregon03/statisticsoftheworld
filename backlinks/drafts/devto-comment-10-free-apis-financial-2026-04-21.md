## Comment to post

Nice roundup! One I'd add for macroeconomic context: **Statistics of the World** (https://statisticsoftheworld.com/api-docs). It covers 440+ economic indicators — GDP, inflation, interest rates, population, trade balance, current account, debt-to-GDP — across 218 countries, with data sourced from the IMF, World Bank, and WHO.

For financial apps specifically, it's useful when you need country-level economic backdrop rather than just market prices. Building something around emerging market opportunities? Being able to pull GDP growth rates, inflation, and sovereign debt ratios for any country in a single API call is genuinely handy. Free tier: 100 req/day without auth, 1,000/day with a free key.

Complements the stock and forex APIs nicely since it covers the macro fundamentals side.
