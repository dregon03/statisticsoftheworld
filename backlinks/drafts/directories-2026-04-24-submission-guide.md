**Long description:**
Statistics of the World is a free data platform and API that normalizes economic and demographic data from the IMF, World Bank, WHO, FRED, and UN into a consistent JSON format. It covers 218 countries across 440+ indicators including GDP (nominal and PPP), inflation, unemployment, population, life expectancy, literacy rate, government debt, trade balance, and more. The free API requires no authentication for 100 requests/day; a free API key allows 1,000/day. The site also includes an interactive country comparison tool, country profiles, and ranked indicator tables.
